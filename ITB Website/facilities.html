Computer Labs



ITB contains a handful of labs available for students. Such labs include "Mac Labs" which is most used by Creative Digital Media students and is sometimes also accessed by Marketing students.

ITB Gym



You can join our Gym by filling out a health screening form at the Sports Office. Membership is free for all students.

Fitness and personal training programmes are available on request. As part of the overall ITB Sports Service ITB provide a number of free and subsidised fitness classes. These change on a semester basis.

Polytunnel



This facility is used by Horticulture students. it's used for growing plants that aren't suited for outdoors in the Irish climate and for starting seedlings.
